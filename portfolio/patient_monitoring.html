Intelligent patient monitoring solution

This project presents the design and construction of an intelligent system for the monitoring of vital signs, which, from a pulse oximeter, will be able to measure and show the arterial blood oxygen saturation, heart rate and temperature, continuously and non-invasively. In addition, the patient's data will be stored in a database on the server, and if some of the vital signs show values out of range, an alarm will be activated and sent to a remote device.

Finally, to demonstrate the applicability of the pulse oximeter in certain disease research areas, a study of the possible relationship between heart rate variability changes and Parkinson's disease has been carried out.
